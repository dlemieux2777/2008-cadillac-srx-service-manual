Correct Sealing Strategy Notice



Correct Sealing Strategy Notice

Notice: In order to maintain proper component alignment the correct sealing strategy must be used when servicing this component. Using a sealing method that was not used with the original component will cause mis-alignment of components and cause engine and/or drivetrain/driveline damage.
