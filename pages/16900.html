Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent)



Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent)

Removal Procedure

1. Move the rear sunroof window to the vent position.
2. Remove the headliner. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .

Important: Do not damage the rear vent window seal when removing the screws.






3. Remove the six hinge-to-frame screws (1).
4. Remove the two outer lift cam arm-to-window shouldered screws on each side.
5. Remove the window from the vehicle.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the rear vent window to the sunroof frame (2).

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Loosely install the six hinge screws.
Important: Set the gap from the motor/actuator compartment window to the vent window at 8 mm (0.3 in)




Tighten the screws to 5 N.m (44 lb in).

3. Install the exterior shouldered cam to the window screws.

Tighten the screws to 5 N.m (44 lb in).

4. Install the headliner. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
5. Verify the operation of the window.
