Hood Stop: Service and Repair



Hood Bumper Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Open the hood.




2. Remove the front hood bumpers from vehicle by turning counterclockwise.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the hood bumpers to the opening in the upper tie bar.
2. Turn the hood bumpers clockwise until the height (A) of 16 mm (5/8 in) is achieved.
