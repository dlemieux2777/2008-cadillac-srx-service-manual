High Intensity Discharge Headlamp Ballast Replacement



High Intensity Discharge Headlamp Ballast Replacement

Removal Procedure


Caution: The high intensity discharge system produces high voltage and current. To reduce the risk of severe shocks and burns:

* Never open the high intensity discharge system ballast or the arc tube assembly starter.
* Never probe between the high intensity discharge system ballast output connector and the arc tube assembly.



Caution: The high intensity discharge system produces high voltage and current. In order to reduce the risk of severe shocks and burns, the battery negative cable must be disconnected any time service work is being performed on or around the high intensity discharge system.

Caution: Refer to Battery Disconnect Caution (Battery Disconnect Caution) .




1. Remove the headlamp. Refer to Headlamp Replacement (Service and Repair) .
2. Remove the screws (1) from the headlamp.





Important: Be careful when disconnecting the electrical connectors.


3. Disconnect the electrical connectors (2).




4. Remove ballast (1) from headlamp.



Installation Procedure






Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .


Important: Always replace the old gasket with the gasket provided with the new ballast .


1. Install the ballast (2) to the headlamp.

Tighten the ballast screws (1) to 2 N.m (18 lb in).

2. Install the headlamp. Refer to Headlamp Replacement (Service and Repair) .
