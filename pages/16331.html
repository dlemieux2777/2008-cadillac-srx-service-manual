Radio Rear Speaker Replacement (Outer)



Radio Rear Speaker Replacement (Outer)

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the body side trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .




2. Remove the fasteners (2) securing the rear speaker.
3. Disconnect the electrical connector.
4. Remove the speaker.



Installation Procedure





1. Locate the speaker onto the studs (1) on the vehicle floor.
2. Connect the electrical connector.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

3. Install the fasteners (2) securing the speaker.

Tighten the fasteners to 9 N.m (80 lb in).

4. Install the body side trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .
