Overhead Console Courtesy Lamp Replacement



Overhead Console Courtesy Lamp Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the front overhead console. Refer to Roof Console Replacement (Service and Repair) .




2. Turn the bulb (2) socket in order to remove it out of the light housing.
3. Remove the bulb.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the bulb into the bulb socket (2).
2. Turn the bulb (2) socket in order to fasten it into the light housing.
3. Install the front overhead console. Refer to Roof Console Replacement (Service and Repair) .
