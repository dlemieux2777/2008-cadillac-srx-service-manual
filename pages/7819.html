Antenna Control Module: Service and Repair



Television Antenna Module Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the right rear body side trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .




2. Remove the nuts (1) securing the module to the vehicle.
3. Disconnect the electrical connectors.
4. Remove the module.



Installation Procedure





1. Connect the electrical connectors.
2. Locate the module the vehicle.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

3. Install the nuts (1) securing the processor to the vehicle.

Tighten the nuts to 9 N.m (80 lb in).

4. Install the right rear body side trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .
