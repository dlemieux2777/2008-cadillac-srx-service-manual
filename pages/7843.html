Rear Seat Number 2 Back Pivot Actuator Motor Replacement - Cushion



Rear Seat Number 2 Back Pivot Actuator Motor Replacement - Cushion

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the seat cushion frame. Refer to Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement (Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement) .




2. Disconnect the drive cables from the horizontal motor (1).
3. Disconnect the electrical connector from the motor.
4. Remove the motor.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the horizontal seat motor (1) to the frame.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the horizontal seat motor screws.

Tighten the screws to 10 N.m (88 lb in).

3. Install the cables into the motor.
4. Connect the electrical connector to the seat motor.
5. Install the seat cushion frame. Refer to Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement (Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement) .
6. Calibrate the seat. Refer to Power Folding Seat Calibration (Programming and Relearning) .
