Automatic Level Control Air Compressor Air Filter Replacement



Automatic Level Control Air Compressor Air Filter Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Raise and support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle (Service and Repair) .
2. Remove the compressor assembly. Refer to Air Compressor Replacement (Air Compressor Replacement) .




3. Remove the hose (1) from the air dryer (2).




4. Remove the mounting screw (1).




5. Rotate the air dryer (1) to remove from compressor.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the air dryer (1) to the compressor.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .





2. Install the air dryer mounting screw (1).

Tighten the bolts to 6 N.m (53 lb in).





3. Install the hose (1) onto the air dryer (2).
4. Install the compressor to the vehicle. Refer to Air Compressor Replacement (Air Compressor Replacement) .
5. Lower the vehicle.
