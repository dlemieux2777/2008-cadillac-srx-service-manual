Idler Pulley: Service and Repair



Drive Belt Idler Pulley Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the air conditioning, power steering and water pump drive belt. Refer to Water Pump and Air Conditioning Compressor and Power Steering Pump Belt Replacement (Water Pump and Air Conditioning Compressor and Power Steering Pump Belt Replacement) .




2. Remove the drive belt idler pulley bolt.
3. Remove the drive belt idler pulley.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the drive belt idler pulley.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the drive belt idler pulley bolt.

Tighten the drive belt idler pulley bolt to 50 N.m (37 lb ft).

3. Install the drive belt. Refer to Water Pump and Air Conditioning Compressor and Power Steering Pump Belt Replacement (Water Pump and Air Conditioning Compressor and Power Steering Pump Belt Replacement) .
