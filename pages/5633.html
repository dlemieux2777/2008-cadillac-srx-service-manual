Front Side Door Window Belt Outer Sealing Strip Replacement



Front Side Door Window Belt Outer Sealing Strip Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the outside rearview mirror. Refer to Power Mirror Replacement (Power Mirror Replacement) .
2. Lower the window to the full down position.





Important: Do not distort the sealing strip.


3. Remove the front door outer belt sealing strip from the door pinch-weld flange.

1. Lift upwards, starting at the rear, to disengage the sealing strip clips.
2. Slide the sealing strip rearward in order to clear the upper auxiliary molding.




Installation Procedure





1. Install the front door outer belt sealing strip to the door pinch-weld flange.

1. Align the sealing strip to the door.
2. Slide the strip forward and under the upper auxiliary molding.
3. Ensure that the end cap is flush to the door cutline.
4. Rotate the sealing strip downward.
5. Align the sealing strip clips to the slots in the door pinch-weld flange.
6. Push the sealing strip downward to fully seat and secure the strip to the door.

2. Install the outside rearview mirror. Refer to Power Mirror Replacement (Power Mirror Replacement) .
