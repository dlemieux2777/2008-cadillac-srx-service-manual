Liftgate Strut Replacement



Liftgate Strut Replacement

Removal Procedure

Caution: When a hood hold open device is being removed or installed, provide alternate support to avoid the possibility of damage to the vehicle or personal injury.


Notice: Apply pressure only at the end of the liftgate assist rod that you are removing or attaching. Do NOT apply pressure to the middle of the rod because damage or bending will result.






1. Open and support the liftgate.
2. Remove the tail lamp. Refer to Tail Lamp Replacement (Service and Repair) .
3. Using a small flat-bladed tool, release the clip retaining the liftgate assist rod to the lower assist rod ball stud.
4. Using a small flat-bladed tool, release the clip retaining the liftgate assist rod to the upper assist rod ball stud.
5. Remove the liftgate assist rod from the vehicle.



Installation Procedure





1. Press the liftgate assist rod onto the upper liftgate assist rod ball stud.
2. Press the liftgate assist rod onto the lower assist rod ball stud.
3. Ensure that both clips are fully seated on the liftgate assist rod.
4. Install the tail lamp. Refer to Tail Lamp Replacement (Service and Repair) .
5. Close the liftgate.
