Windshield Upper Reveal Molding Replacement



Windshield Upper Reveal Molding Replacement

The windshield reveal molding is a one piece molded design. The reveal molding is adhesive bonded to the windshield and cannot be removed for replacement. Refer to Windshield Replacement (Windshield Replacement) if the molding becomes damaged.
