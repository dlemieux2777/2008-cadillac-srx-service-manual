Wiring Harness Removal



Wiring Harness Removal





1. Using a small screwdriver pry the wiring harness off of the following components:

* Pressure Control Solenoid (357)
* TCC PWM Solenoid (352)
* 1-2 Shift Solenoid (368)
* 2-3 Shift Solenoid (369)
* 4-5 Shift Solenoid (376)
* Manual Shift Shaft Switch (602)
* Input Speed Sensor (44)
* Output Speed Sensor (42)





2. Remove the connector retainer (54) from the case.




3. Remove the wiring harness (55) from the case.
