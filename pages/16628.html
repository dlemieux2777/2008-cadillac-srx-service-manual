Front Door Window Regulator: Service and Repair



Front Side Door Window Regulator Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Raise the window to the full up position.
2. Remove the door trim panel. Refer to Front Side Door Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
3. Remove the water deflector. Refer to Front Side Door Water Deflector Replacement (Service and Repair) .
4. Raise and support the window in the full up position.
5. Through the access holes in the inner door panel, loosen the window clamp nuts 4 revolutions.
6. Connect the door trim panel wiring harness.
7. Lower the window regulator 3/4 of the way down.
8. Disconnect the electrical connector from the window regulator motor.
9. Remove the window regulator bolts (1, 2, 3, 4, 5).
10. Remove the window regulator from the door.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the window regulator into the door.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the window regulator bolts (1, 2, 3, 4, 5).

Tighten the bolts to 9 N.m (80 lb in).

3. Remove the support from the window.
4. Lower the window into the window regulator clamps.
The window must be fully rearward into the window channel when it is seated in the window clamps.
5. Connect the power window motor electrical connector.

Important: Start the vehicle to ensure maximum power to the window motor. This is required to fully seat the window for adjustment.


6. Start the vehicle.
7. Raise the window to the full up position.

Tighten the window clamp nuts to 11 N.m (95 lb in).

8. Inspect the window for proper operation and fit.
9. Shut off the vehicle.
10. Install the water deflector. Refer to Front Side Door Water Deflector Replacement (Service and Repair) .
11. Install the door trim panel. Refer to Front Side Door Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
12. Reprogramming the power window regulator. Refer to Window Regulator Motor Programming and Setup (Programming and Relearning) .
