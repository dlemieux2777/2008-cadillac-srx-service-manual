Rear Side Door Upper Auxiliary Weatherstrip Replacement



Rear Side Door Upper Auxiliary Weatherstrip Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Lower the window to the full down position.
2. Remove the rear door stationary window/division channel. Refer to Rear Side Door Stationary Window and Front Channel Replacement (Rear Side Door Stationary Window and Front Channel Replacement) .




3. Remove the screws from the rear door upper auxiliary weatherstrip.
4. Remove the upper auxiliary weatherstrip.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the rear door upper auxiliary weatherstrip onto the door frame.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice .

2. Install the screws working from the front to the rear.

Tighten the screws working from the front to the rear to 2 N.m (18 lb in).

3. Install the rear door stationary window/division channel. Refer to Rear Side Door Stationary Window and Front Channel Replacement (Rear Side Door Stationary Window and Front Channel Replacement) .
