Sunroof Window Seal Replacement (Rear)



Sunroof Window Seal Replacement (Rear)

Removal Procedure





1. Remove the rear venting and/or stationary window panel. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
2. Pull up on the rear window seal (2). Remove the window seal from the channel.



Installation Procedure





1. Clean the silicone adhesive from the window seal groove.
2. Use a caulk gun to apply fresh silicone RTV sealant P/N 12346192 (Canadian P/N 10953476) to the new seal (2).
3. Install the seal (2) to the groove with a small roller or thumb pressure (1).
4. Install the rear window panel. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
