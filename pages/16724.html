Rear Carpet Retainer Replacement



Rear Carpet Retainer Replacement

Tools Required

J 38778 Door Trim Pad Clip Remover


Removal Procedure





1. Using a J 38778 release the retainer clips from the rocker panel.
2. Tilt the carpet retainer inward and pull downward in order to release the trim from the retainer at the rear of the carpet retainer (1).
3. Remove the rear carpet retainer.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the rear carpet retainer (1) with the slots in the rocker.
2. Tilt the trim plate in order to align the retainer at the rear of the carpet retainer.
3. Position the retainers into the slots on the rocker panel.
4. Press downward in order to engage the retainers into the slots in the rocker panel.
