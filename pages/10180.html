Door Control Module Replacement



Door Control Module Replacement

The door control module is not serviced separately. The door control module is part of the front door switch assembly. The door control module and the front door switch assembly must be replaced as an assembly. Refer to Door Lock and Side Window Switch Replacement - Driver Side (Service and Repair) or Door Lock and Side Window Switch Replacement - Passenger Side (Service and Repair) .
