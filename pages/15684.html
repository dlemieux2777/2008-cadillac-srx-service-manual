Rear Suspension



Stabilizer Shaft Insulator Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Raise and support the vehicle. Refer to Lifting and Jacking the Vehicle (Service and Repair) .
2. Remove the stabilizer shaft mounting bolts.
3. Remove the stabilizer shaft bracket.
4. Remove the stabilizer shaft insulator from the stabilizer shaft.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the stabilizer shaft insulator to the stabilizer shaft with the slit facing forwards.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the stabilizer shaft bracket.
3. Install the stabilizer shaft bracket mounting bolts.

Tighten the bolts to 60 N.m (44 lb ft).

4. Lower the vehicle.
