Rear Seat Back Cushion Frame Replacement - Right Side



Rear Seat Back Cushion Frame Replacement - Right Side

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the seat. Refer to Rear Seat Replacement (Rear Seat Replacement) .
2. Remove the right seat back. Refer to Rear Seat Number 1 Back Replacement - Right Side (Rear Seat Number 1 Back Replacement - Right Side) .
3. Remove the seat back cover and pad. Refer to Rear Seat Back Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement - Right Side (Rear Seat Back Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement - Right Side) .
4. Remove the latch release assembly. Refer to Rear Number 1 Seat Back Release Latch Replacement (Rear Number 1 Seat Back Release Latch Replacement) .
5. Remove the head restraint. Refer to Rear Seat Head Restraint Replacement (Rear Seat Head Restraint Replacement) .
6. Remove the head restraint guides. Refer to Rear Seat Head Restraint Guide Replacement (Rear Seat Head Restraint Guide Replacement) .



Installation Procedure

1. Install the seat back cover and pad. Refer to Rear Seat Back Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement - Right Side (Rear Seat Back Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement - Right Side) .
2. Install the latch release assembly. Refer to Rear Number 1 Seat Back Release Latch Replacement (Rear Number 1 Seat Back Release Latch Replacement) .
3. Install the head restraint guides. Refer to Rear Seat Head Restraint Guide Replacement (Rear Seat Head Restraint Guide Replacement) .
4. Install the head restraint. Refer to Rear Seat Head Restraint Replacement (Rear Seat Head Restraint Replacement) .
5. Install the right seat back. Refer to Rear Seat Number 1 Back Replacement - Right Side (Rear Seat Number 1 Back Replacement - Right Side) .
6. Install the seat. Refer to Rear Seat Replacement (Rear Seat Replacement) .
