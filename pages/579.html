4. Drive Belt Removal



Drive Belt Removal

Outer Drive Belt





1. Remove the radiator hose bracket bolt.
2. Remove the radiator hose bracket.




3. Insert a 1/2-inch drive extension into the drive belt tensioner.
4. Push down on the extension to release tension.
5. Remove the drive belt from the power steering pump.
6. Slowly return the drive belt tensioner to the original position.




7. Remove the outer drive belt.



Inner Drive Belt





1. Insert a 1/2-inch drive extension into the drive belt tensioner.
2. Push down on the extension to release tension.
3. Remove the drive belt from the generator.
4. Slowly return the drive belt tensioner to the original position.




5. Remove the inner drive belt.
