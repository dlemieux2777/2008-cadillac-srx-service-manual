Sunroof Window Seal Replacement (Center)



Sunroof Window Seal Replacement (Center)

Removal Procedure





1. Remove the center sunroof window. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
2. Pull up on the window seal (2). Remove the window seal from the channel.



Installation Procedure





1. Use thumb pressure (1) or a small roller tool to install the window seal.
2. Install the center sunroof window. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
