76. Oil Pan Disassemble



Oil Pan Disassemble





1. Drill out the oil pan gasket rivets.




2. Remove and discard the oil pan gasket.




3. Remove the oil pan drain plug.
4. Remove and discard the oil pan drain plug O-ring.
