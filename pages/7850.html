Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement



Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Cover and Pad Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the seat from the vehicle. Refer to Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement (Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement) .
2. Lay the seat down on the seat back.




3. Disconnect the J channel retainers (1) from the sides of the seat cover.




4. Remove the seat cover (1) from the seat bottom.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the seat cover (1) on to the seat bottom.




2. Connect all J channel retainers (1).
3. Install the seat into the vehicle. Refer to Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement (Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement) .
