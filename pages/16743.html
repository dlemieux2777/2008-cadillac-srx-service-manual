Roof Console Replacement



Roof Console Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Using your fingers, gently pull downward at the front edge of the overhead console (1).
2. Disconnect the electrical connectors.
3. Remove the overhead console.



Installation Procedure





1. Connect the electrical connectors.
2. Install the overhead console into the headliner opening.
3. Ensure the locking tabs are properly positioned.
4. Press upward on the overhead console (1) to secure the locking tabs to the console bracket.
