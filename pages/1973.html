Transmission Fluid Draining



Transmission Fluid Draining

Tools Required

J 45012 Holding Fixture





1. Put transmission in park.
2. Install J 45012 , to hold the prop shaft flange (31).

Important: Discard retainer (30), seal (32), washer (33) and nut (34).


3. Remove the prop shaft flange nut (34).
4. Remove the prop shaft flange washer (33) and seal (32).
5. Remove the prop shaft flange (31) and the output shaft assembly retainer (30).
6. Slowly rotate the transmission to a vertical position, with the bell housing facing up, and allow any excess fluid to drain.
