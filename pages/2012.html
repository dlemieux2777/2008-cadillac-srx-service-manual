Direct and Reverse and Forward and Coast Clutch Installation






Notice: Do not damage the input shaft fluid seals when removing or installing the direct and reverse coast clutch assembly.

1. Place the direct and reverse clutch assembly (6) onto the forward and coast clutch assembly (7).




2. Install the direct and reverse clutch assembly (6) and the forward and coast clutch assembly (7) into the case.
