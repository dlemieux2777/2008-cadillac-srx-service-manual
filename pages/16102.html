Seat Belt Retractor Replacement - No. 2 Rear



Seat Belt Retractor Replacement - No. 2 Rear

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the body side rear upper garnish molding. Refer to Body Side Rear Upper Garnish Molding Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Upper Garnish Molding Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .
2. Remove the body side rear trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .




3. Remove the bolt that retains the seat belt retractor assembly (2) to the body.
4. Remove the seat belt retractor assembly from the vehicle.



Installation Procedure






Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

1. Install the seat belt retractor assembly (2) to the vehicle.

Tighten the rear seat belt retractor bolts to 70 N.m (52 lb ft).

2. Install the body side rear trim panel. Refer to Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Trim Panel Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .
3. Insert the seat belt through the body side rear upper garnish molding.
4. Install the rear seat belt upper guide bolt (1). Ensure that the seat belt webbing is not twisted.

Tighten the bolt to 70 N.m (52 lb ft).

5. Install the body side rear upper garnish molding. Refer to Body Side Rear Upper Garnish Molding Replacement (w/TB5) (Service and Repair)Body Side Rear Upper Garnish Molding Replacement (w/o TB5) (Service and Repair) .
