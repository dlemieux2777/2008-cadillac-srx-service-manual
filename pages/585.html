10. Water Outlet Housing Removal



Water Outlet Housing Removal





1. Remove the EVAP purge valve tube from the EVAP purge valve.




2. Remove the throttle body bolts.
3. Remove the throttle body from the water outlet.




4. Remove and discard the throttle body gasket.




5. Loosen the clamp (1) on the intake manifold plenum duct.




6. Remove the water outlet housing bolts.
7. Remove the water outlet housing.
