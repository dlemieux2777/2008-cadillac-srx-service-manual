Extension Housing Removal



Extension Housing Removal





1. Remove the case extension bolts (35).
2. Remove the case extension (27).
3. Remove the case extension gasket (25).
4. Remove the input and reaction carrier thrust washer (22) and bearing (23) - some models, or thrust washer (67) and thrust bearing (69).
