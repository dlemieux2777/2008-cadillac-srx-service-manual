Input and Reaction Carrier Disassemble



Input and Reaction Carrier Disassemble





1. Remove the snap ring (540).
2. Remove the input and reaction bearing (541).
3. Remove the 2nd clutch roller inner race bearing (542).
4. Remove the 2nd clutch sprag (549).
5. Remove the reaction internal gear flange washer (551).
