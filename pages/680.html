100. Oil Pump Suction Pipe and Screen Assembly Installation



Oil Pump Suction Pipe and Screen Assembly Installation





1. Install NEW oil pump pipe gasket on oil pump pipe.





Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the oil pump pick-up retaining bolt and nut.

* Tighten the oil pump pick-up tube nut to 24 N.m (18 lb ft).
* Tighten the oil pump pick-up tube bolt to 10 N.m (89 lb in).
