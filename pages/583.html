8. Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor Removal



Air Conditioning (A/C) Compressor Removal





1. Remove the A/C compressor bolts.
2. Remove the A/C compressor.




3. Remove the P/S pump and A/C compressor bracket front bolts.




4. Remove the P/S pump and A/C compressor bracket side bolts.
5. Remove the P/S pump and A/C compressor bracket.
