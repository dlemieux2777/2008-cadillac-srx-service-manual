87. Intake Manifold Disassemble



Intake Manifold Disassemble






Important: Do not reuse the intake manifold-to-cylinder head sealing gaskets.


1. Remove and discard the intake manifold-to-cylinder head gaskets.




2. Remove the sight shield retainer ball studs.




3. Remove the manifold vacuum source cap.




4. Remove the vacuum boost fitting.
