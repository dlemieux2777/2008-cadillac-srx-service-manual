Front Upper Speaker Replacement



Front Upper Speaker Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Remove the windshield garnish molding (2). Refer to Windshield Pillar Garnish Molding Replacement (Service and Repair) .
2. Disconnect the electrical connector (1).
3. Release the retaining tabs to remove the speaker from the garnish molding.
4. Remove the speaker.



Installation Procedure





1. Reinstall the speaker to the trim panel.
2. Push the speaker into the retaining tabs to secure it to the garnish molding.
3. Connect the electrical connector (1).
4. Install the windshield garnish molding (2) to the vehicle. Refer to Windshield Pillar Garnish Molding Replacement (Service and Repair) .
