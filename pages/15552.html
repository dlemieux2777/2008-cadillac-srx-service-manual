Front Caster and Camber Adjustment



Front Caster and Camber Adjustment


Tools Required
* J 45845 Caster and Camber Adjusting Tool
* J-45845-10 Caster and Camber Adjusting Rod






1. Install the J 45845 or the J-45845-10 to the lower control arm and the frame.
2. Loosen the lower control arm to frame bolt.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

3. Adjust the camber angle by turning the turnbuckle on J 45845 or the J-45845-10 until the specifications have been met.

Tighten the bolt to 135 N.m (96 lb ft).

4. Verify that the camber is still with specifications. Refer to Wheel Alignment Specifications (Specifications) .
5. Adjust the rear toe. Refer to Rear Toe Adjustment (Service and Repair) .
