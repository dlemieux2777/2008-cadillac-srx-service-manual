Sunroof Sunshade Assembly Replacement (Rear)



Sunroof Sunshade Assembly Replacement (Rear)

Removal Procedure

1. Open the rear sunshade (5) to the full open/forward position.
2. It is only necessary to lower the headliner. Only do those step in headliner replacement that will lower the headliner enough to gain access to the part. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
3. Remove the rear sunroof vent window. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
Important: Prior to releasing the sunshade track retainers from the sunshade cable barrels, ensure that the sunshade is at the full open position.







4. Using a medium flat-bladed tool, release the sunroof/sunshade screws (2) from the cable assembly (4).




5. Remove the screws (2) securing the sunshade assembly (3) to the lower sunroof frame assembly.
6. With the sunshade assembly screws removed, gently move forward one corner of the assembly in order to release the sunshade from the rear track assembly.
7. Remove the sunshade assembly from the vehicle.



Installation Procedure

1. Position the sunroof/sunshade assembly to the rear sunroof track assembly.
Important: Install one side of the sunroof/sunshade first, then move the other side rearward in order to locate the sunshade retainer into the front track channel.







2. Prior to installing the sunshade assembly to the bottom of the sunroof frame, pull the sunshade material (1) approximately 75 mm (3 in) outward from the assembly (2). Pull the material outward.
3. Position the sunroof/sunshade assembly to the sunroof frame.





Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

4. Install the sunroof/sunshade assembly screws (2).

Tighten the 6 screws to 5 N.m (44 lb in).





5. Pull the sunshade material outward. Install the sunshade screws (1) into the sunshade channel (2).




6. Use downward thumb (1) pressure to press on the retainer (2) until the retainer locks over the sunshade cable barrel (4).
7. Install the headliner. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
8. Install the rear sunroof vent window. Refer to Sunroof Window Replacement (Front) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Vent) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Center) (Service and Repair)Sunroof Window Replacement (Rear Stationary) (Service and Repair) .
9. Perform the rear vent window sunshade initialization process. Refer to Sunroof Rear Window Initialization Process (Sunroof Rear Window Initialization Process) .
