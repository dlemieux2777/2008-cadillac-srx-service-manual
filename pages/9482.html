Capacity Specifications



DIFFERENTIAL FLUID CAPACITY

FRONT DIFFERENTIAL
Fluid Capacity 0.7 liters (0.74 quarts)

REAR DIFFERENTIAL
OPEN DIFFERENTIAL
Drain and Fill with cover removed 1.3 liters (1.37 quarts)
Drain and Fill with use of drain plug 1.1 liters (1.16 quarts)

LIMITED SLIP DIFFERENTIAL
Drain and Fill with cover removed 1.2 liters (1.27 quarts)
Drain and Fill with use of drain plug 1.0 liters (1.06 quarts)

LIMITED SLIP ADDITIVE

Important: The Limited Slip Additive container capacity is greater than the amount needed. Only add the amount specified.

Drain and Fill with cover removed 100 ml (3.38 ounces)
Drain and Fill with use of drain plug 70 ml (2.37 ounces)

NOTE: All capacities are approximate. When adding, be sure to fill to the approximate level. Recheck fluid level after filling.
