3. Exhaust Manifold Removal - Right Side



Exhaust Manifold Removal - Right Side (AWD)





1. Remove the right exhaust manifold heat shield bolts.
2. Remove the right exhaust manifold heat shield.




3. Remove the right exhaust manifold bolts from the right cylinder head and bell housing.
4. Remove the right exhaust manifold.




5. Remove the right exhaust manifold gasket.




6. Remove the right exhaust manifold studs from the right cylinder head.




7. Remove the right engine mount upper nut.
8. Remove the right engine mount.




9. Remove the right engine mount bracket bolts.
10. Remove the right engine mount bracket.
