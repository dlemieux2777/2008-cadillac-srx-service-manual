Without Oil Cooler



Oil Filter Adapter Installation (Without Oil Cooler)





1. Install a NEW gasket to the oil filter adapter.




2. Position the oil filter adapter on the crankcase.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

3. Install the oil filter adapter bolts.

Tighten the oil filter adapter bolts to 25 N.m (18 lb ft).





4. Fill the oil filter with oil.
5. Install the oil filter.

Tighten the oil filter to 32 N.m (24 lb ft).
