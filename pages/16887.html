Sunroof Drain Inspection and Cleaning (Rear Vent Window)

Inspection and Cleaning Procedure

1. Move the rear window to the vent position.
2. Pour a small amount of water into the sunroof module drain trough. Inspect for a plugged or disconnected drainage system. If the water flow is restricted, perform the following steps:

Caution: Refer to Safety Glasses and Compressed Air Caution (Safety Glasses and Compressed Air Warning) .

Important: Use care when using compressed air to clear the sunroof drain hose. Do not exceed 241 kPa (35 psi).


* Use a flexible wire or 241 kPa (35 psi) of compressed air to clear any material plugging the drain system.
* Inspect the drainage system for proper water drainage.
* If water drainage is still not adequate, remove the headliner. Inspect the sunroof drain hoses for proper routing. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .

3. Install the rear headliner, if necessary. Refer to Headlining Trim Panel Replacement (Service and Repair) .
