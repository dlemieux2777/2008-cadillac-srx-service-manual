21. Ignition Coil Module Removal - Right Side



Ignition Coil Module Removal - Right Side





1. Remove the ignition coil assembly cover.




2. Remove the ignition coil assembly bolts (708) and ball studs (709).
3. Carefully, remove the ignition coil assembly.




4. Remove the spark plugs.
