Hood Latch: Adjustments



Hood Latch Adjustment

Important: Ensure that you have the proper alignment of the hood to the body before you tighten the hood latch mounting fasteners under the following conditions:


* After removing the hood latch assembly

* After loosening the mounting bolts

* After changing the hood adjustment








1. With the hood latch attaching fasteners (1) loose, slowly lower the hood onto the latch (2) in order to achieve the proper cross vehicle adjustment of the hood latch.
2. Raise the hood.

Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice .

3. Tighten the fasteners.

Tighten the fasteners (1) to 9 N.m (80 lb in).
