Front Side Door Bottom Auxiliary Sealing Strip Replacement



Front Side Door Bottom Auxiliary Sealing Strip Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. With the door open, remove the front door bottom sealing strip retainers.
2. Remove the sealing strip.



Installation Procedure





1. Install the front door bottom sealing strip to the inside lower door edge.
2. Secure the sealing strip by inserting the retainers into the holes in the lower door panel.
