Direct and Reverse Clutch Disassemble



Direct and Reverse Clutch Disassemble


Tools Required
* J 38734 Intermediate Spring Compressor Adapter
* J 45139 Compressor Plate






1. Remove the reverse clutch backing plate retainer (420).
2. Remove the backing plate (419).
3. Remove the clutch plates (418, 417). Refer to Clutch Plate Selection Specifications (Clutch Plate Selection Specifications) .
4. Remove the apply plate (416).
5. Remove the reverse clutch spacer (415).




6. Remove the direct clutch backing plate retainer ring (414).
7. Remove the backing plate (413).
8. Remove the clutch plates (412, 411). Refer to Clutch Plate Selection Specifications (Clutch Plate Selection Specifications) .
9. Remove the apply plate (410).




10. Use J 38734 and J 45139 to compress the direct and reverse piston spring.
11. Remove the direct and reverse clutch spring retainer ring (409).
12. Remove J 38734 and J 45139 .




13. Remove the spring retainer (408).
14. Remove the spring (407).





Important: It may be necessary to use air pressure to remove the piston.


15. Remove the direct clutch piston (406).




16. Remove the reverse clutch piston (404).
17. Remove the reverse clutch piston inner seal (402).
