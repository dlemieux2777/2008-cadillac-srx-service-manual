Electronic Suspension Control Module (ESCM) Replacement/Reprogramming

If the electronic suspension control module (ESCM) is replaced, the following procedures must be performed:

* ESCM programming-Refer to Service Programming System (SPS) (Programming and Relearning) .
* Revise the trim set calibration using the scan tool ALC Trimset Special Function. Refer to Suspension Position Calibration (Programming and Relearning) .
