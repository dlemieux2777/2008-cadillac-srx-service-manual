Hood Insulator / Pad: Service and Repair



Hood Insulator Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Open the hood.
2. Remove the push-in retainers retaining the hood insulator to the hood.
3. Remove the hood insulator from the hood.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the hood insulator to the hood.
2. Install the push-in retainers in order to secure the hood insulator to the hood.
3. Close the hood.
