Sound Proofing / Insulation: Service and Repair



Front Fender Insulator Replacement

Removal Procedure





1. Open the front door.
2. Remove the push-in retainers securing the front fender sound insulator to the vehicle.
3. Remove the front fender sound insulator from the vehicle.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the front fender sound insulator to the vehicle.
2. Install the push-in fasteners in order to secure the front fender sound insulator to the vehicle.
3. Close the door.
