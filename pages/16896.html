Sunroof / Moonroof Panel: Adjustments



Sunroof Window Height and Opening Fit Adjustment

The Ultra View full sunroof utilizes a non-adjustable sunroof window design. The front slider and the rear venting windows are designed without any adjustments.


When the windows are removed and then re-initialized, all moveable component parts are designed for exact fit and function. Refer to Sunroof Rear Window Initialization Process (Sunroof Rear Window Initialization Process) or to Sunroof Front Window/Sunshade Initialization Process (Sunroof Front Window/Sunshade Initialization Process) .
