Fuel Door: Service and Repair



Fuel Tank Filler Door Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Open the fuel filler door.




2. Drill out the rivets from the fuel filler door.
3. Remove the fuel filler door from the quarter panel.



Installation Procedure





1. Position the fuel filler door to the quarter panel.
2. Use new rivets to install the fuel filler door to the quarter panel.
3. Close the fuel filler door.
