Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement



Rear Seat Number 2 Cushion Frame Replacement

Removal Procedure

1. Remove the seat from the vehicle. Refer to Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement (Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement) .
2. Lay the seat down on the seat back.




3. Disconnect the J-channel retainers (1) from the sides of the seat cover.




4. Remove the seat cover (1) from the seat bottom.




5. Remove the recliner motor screws (1) from the seat frame.




6. Remove the screws from the horizontal motor (1).




7. Remove the seat frame bolts (1).
8. Remove the seat frame.



Installation Procedure

1. Place the seat frame onto the seat adjuster.





Notice: Refer to Fastener Notice (Fastener Notice) .

2. Install the seat frame retaining bolts (1).

Tighten the bolts to 35 N.m (26 lb ft).





3. Install the horizontal motor (1).

Tighten the horizontal motor screws to 10 N.m (88 lb in).





4. Install the recliner motor to the seat frame.

Tighten the recliner motor screws (1) to 10 N.m (88 lb in).





5. Install the seat cover (1) on to the seat bottom.




6. Connect all J channel retainers (1).
7. Install the seat into the vehicle. Refer to Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement (Rear Number 2 Folding Seat Replacement) .
